My Acheivements

~I have achieved 16 gold medals with other awards as of now:-

-I have won a Silicon Valley, USA trip by creating an App. I have also been selected as a Young Visionary to showcase my idea in the Young Visionaries Event.

-I presented my idea to Mr Sonam Wangchuk, recipient of the prestigious Ramon Magsaysay Award with over 400 patents to his name.

-I am a gold scholar in ASSET Talent Search Examination in Class 5 and again, in Class 6.

-I am awarded with two gold medals in Unified Cyber Olympiad with an AIR (All India Rank) of 15 and 48 respectively. I also won a gold medal in UIMO (Unified International Mathematics Olympiad) in 2021.

-I won two gold medals in Silverzone Olympiad in Computer and General Knowledge.

-I won 6 gold medals in the SOF Olympiad; 3 in Mathematics, 1 in Cyber and another two consecutive medals in General Knowledge (GK) with distinction. I also received a silver medal in Science.

-I have acheived the excellency medal & certificate for the NSTSE exam conducted by the Unified Council Team.

-I have met with Mr Hritihik Roshan to showcase my app and present my ideas about it

-I received a Young Scholar Award by The Times Group in 2019.
